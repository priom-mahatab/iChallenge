Fundraising has never been easier

Many fundraising opportunities offered by companies are buried deep within their websites, making them difficult to find and access. This lack of visibility creates a barrier for nonprofits, student organizations, and community groups seeking support. GROW is a centralized website that aggregates and organizes these fundraising opportunities, making them easily searchable and accessible to organizations in need of funding.
